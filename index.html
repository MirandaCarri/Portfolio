My name is Miranda Carrillo

Welcome to my page! I guess you are here to know a bit about me, huh? Currently, I am a senior Computer Science student at Texas Tech University and graduate in June 2019(Yay!). Believe it or not, but Computer Science was not initially my first chose, and that is because I did not know it was a choice. I had started as a Computer Engineer major, however, I quickly realized working with hardware was not for me. I had always been fascinated by how software was created and simply how it works by everyday softwares. It was when I stumbled onto a simple java programming tutorial. And it had me at "Hello World". From then on, I changed my major and since been increasing my knowlegde in this degree.

I have had two internships: one in Curitiba, Brazil as a Software Developer intern in Summer 2018, and an Associate Software Developer at FundView in Sep-Dec 2018 at Lubbock,TX. During these internships, I had experienced working in Agile methodology and Scrum methodology working in a small team and working individually. Over the course of my college career, I have created a couple of web applications and an IOS application. I am excited to start a career in this field and apply all the knowledge I have learned, as well as learn more in this industry.
